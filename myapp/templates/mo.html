ALL TYPES OF TYRES AVAILABLE

At our tyre shop, we offer a wide range of JK Tyre products to suit all vehicles and needs. From high-performance and long-lasting radial tyres to heavy-duty truck tyres, we’ve got you covered. We stock tubeless, tube-type, and run-flat tyres for bikes, scooters, SUVs, sedans, and commercial vehicles — ensuring the right fit, alignment, and service for a safe and smooth ride.

WHY USE JK TYRES?

  • High Durability – Built to withstand tough roads and long journeys.
  • Excellent Grip – Ensures safety with strong traction in all weather conditions.
  • Fuel Efficiency – Designed to reduce rolling resistance and save fuel.
  • Wide Range – Tyres for bikes, cars, SUVs, trucks, and commercial vehicles.
  • Advanced Technology – Features like tubeless and run-flat designs.
